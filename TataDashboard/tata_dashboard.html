Data Visualisation: Empowering Business with Effective Insights

📘 Project Overview

This project was developed as part of the Tata Group Virtual Internship hosted on Forage. It involves creating business-focused visualisations using Power BI to help leadership make data-driven decisions for expansion and performance improvement.

🎯 Business Objectives

  • Analyze monthly revenue trends (2011 only)
  • Identify top 10 countries by revenue (excluding UK)
  • Determine top 10 customers by revenue
  • Understand country-wise demand to guide expansion

📄 View the Business Requirements Document (BRD) created to capture these objectives and requirements..

🧹 Data Cleaning Summary

  • Removed rows with Quantity < 1
  • Removed rows with Unit Price < 0
  • Removed all rows with null values
  • Created a Revenue column using: Revenue = Quantity * Unit Price

📊 Visualizations & Insights

This project used Power BI to explore sales data and uncover business insights for Tata's online retail division. Key visuals include revenue trends, top-performing countries, customer revenue breakdown, and market demand—designed to support strategic decision-making by the CEO and CMO.
